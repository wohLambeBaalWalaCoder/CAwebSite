What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is an investment strategy where you invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps build wealth over time through rupee cost averaging and the power of compounding.

Benefits of SIP

• Start with as little as ₹500/month
• Benefit from rupee cost averaging
• Build discipline in investing
• Take advantage of compounding
